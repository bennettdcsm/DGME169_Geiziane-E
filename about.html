About Chá-de

Brewing Moments, Not Just Coffee.

Chá-de is a cozy neighborhood cafe created for people who love slow mornings, warm conversations, and thoughtfully crafted drinks. Every detail, from our beans to our playlists, is chosen to brighten your day.

What We’re Good At

Our strengths come from a blend of craft, creativity, and care for our drinks, our space, and our community.

Signature Coffee and Tea

Single-origin espresso, seasonal pour-overs, and a curated tea menu with comforting classics and bold new blends.

Warm, Welcoming Service

We learn names, remember orders, and make space for everyone, from laptop workers to friends catching up.

Thoughtful Design

A cozy interior with soft lighting, natural textures, and music that invites you to stay just a little longer.

Community Events

Latte art nights, local maker pop-ups, and open mic evenings that help our neighborhood connect.
